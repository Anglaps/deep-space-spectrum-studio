# Data Attributions

The spectra bundled in this directory are real, publicly released observations.
They are redistributed here for demonstration under the source surveys' open
data policies. Re-fetch or update them with `node scripts/fetch-spectra.mjs`.

## SDSS DR17

Files: `sdss-*.fits` (SDSS "lite" coadded spectra, DR17).

Source: SDSS Science Archive Server, `https://data.sdss.org/sas/dr17/`.

SDSS data are publicly available and free to use. SDSS asks that the following
acknowledgement accompany work that uses the data:

> Funding for the Sloan Digital Sky Survey has been provided by the Alfred P.
> Sloan Foundation, the U.S. Department of Energy Office of Science, and the
> Participating Institutions. SDSS acknowledges support and resources from the
> Center for High-Performance Computing at the University of Utah. The SDSS
> website is www.sdss.org.

Full policy: https://www.sdss.org/collaboration/citing-sdss/

Note: SDSS is operated by the Astrophysical Research Consortium (NSF / DOE /
participating institutions), not NASA. NASA telescope spectra (HST, JWST) from
the MAST archive are planned for a later data increment; their mission
acknowledgements will be added here when those files are bundled.
